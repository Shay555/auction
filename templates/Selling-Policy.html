  • Listing practices

    For a satisfying and fun experience on this site, we're helping sellers list their items in the most effective ways possible. As general rules, sellers have to:

    1. Select a category that matches the item for sale.
    2. Provide a clear, honest, and accurate description about the item.
    3. Avoid tactics like keyword spamming because they make it hard for people to find what they're looking for.

  • Reporting breaches of selling policies

    If you see a listing that breaches eBay policies, please report it by clicking the Report item link in the listing, or by clicking the Report or Contact Us link located on most of our help pages.

    When a policy breach occurs, we email the seller and the buyer to let them know that the listing has been removed from this site. >
